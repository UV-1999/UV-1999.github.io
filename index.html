About me

A student, a researcher and pretty much a normal guy. I am an aspiring astrophysicist with research interests in interiors of compact stars, computational high-energy astrophysics, observational astronomy, gravitational waves and everything else that astonishes me. I also read about philosophy, psychology, history and evolutionary biology. I have completed my masters in physics and now looking for PhD positions. I also spend time making digital arts, do creative coding and expanding on my personal research journal which I will put on this website soon.
